Cybersquare learning platform

Every registered students will hav a seperate login account to the cyber square online learning platform.Here they will be able to view their course progress,assignments provided by teachers,their batch performance,progress cards and so on.They will be able to attend online exams and live classes.Students can attend the live class to clear their doubts and interact with teachers.Apart from these they will also get notification on upcoming live classes,news,and events so that they won't miss them.

Teachers can also login into cybersquare platform and able to view student performance,batch progress,make reports,evaluate their works such as assaignments,projects,questions,etc.Teachers can shedule an online class and online exams also.

Cybersquare coding courses

Cybersquare provides live b2c online coding courses for kids and prepare them for future.Our experts will provide 1:1 online coding classes and help your child to create their own web sites,apps, and games.
